CHOOSE YOUR WAY

There are almost endless ways to explore Greece. Choose from main quests, story-driven side quests and more as you seek to unearth the mysteries and secrets of this hallowed ground. Your decisions shape the world around you with over 30 hours of choice dialogue and multiple game endings. Experience an entire ancient world that constantly evolves and reacts to your every decision.

BECOME A SPARTAN HERO

Embark on an epic journey that will take you from your humble beginnings as a Spartan mercenary to a legendary Greek hero and discover the truth about your mysterious past as Alexios or Cassandra. Sail to the far reaches of the Aegean Sea, forming alliances and making enemies. Along the way, you'll meet historical figures, mystical characters, and a whole cast of others who will impact your journey.

ANCIENT GREECE AWAITS

Explore an entire country full of wild environments and cities at the height of Greece's Golden Age. Visit Sparta and see Athens in all its glory, walk in the footsteps of legends like Ulysses and Hercules and discover the secrets of Greece. Unexpected encounters will bring your story to life as you encounter multifaceted characters, fight formidable enemies, and much more.

THE ART OF BATTLE

Show off your extraordinary warrior skills and change the tides of battle during one of the deadliest conflicts of the era, the Peloponnesian War. Engage in epic clashes between Sparta and Athens in massive battles with 150x150 soldiers battling each other.
